DELIGHTFUL MOBILE EXPERIENCES

Affordable mobile solutions for your Startup.

We are a San Francisco based mobile design and application development studio.

Our goal is to help startups make the jump to mobile by providing a full cycle app development team at an affordable price. From simple prototypes to pitch investors to a scalable app that will grow with your company, we will help you achieve your goals - pain free.

With limited investment and tough deadlines, startups can fail by taking too long to launch or spending time, energy and money developing products with no proof of concept.

Startup Studio is here support you by developing your first usable and scalable product to help identify product-market fit and push you to the next level.

We specialize in iOS apps using the latest technologies in both languages Objective-C and Swift, we are a diverse team of developers with years of experience in web and Android.

Step 1

Send us an introduction of your project and we will create an initial design of your app and send it back to you - for free!

Step 2

If you like what you get, we will put together a final quote for product design and/or development of your product.

Step 3

We assign you the best team to your project and start building!

What do we do?

Engineering

We craft mobile apps, to turn ideas into real products. Using state-of-the-art technologies, we make sure the project stays modular and scales smoothly. We don't celebrate when we acquire clients, we celebrate when our clients gets positive feedback from theirs.

UX Design

From user experience, wireframes and prototypes to mock-ups, interfaces, and assets, we've got you covered. Through a highly interdisciplinary environment, we build gorgeous interfaces, without losing sight of the engineering aspect.
